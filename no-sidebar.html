Let's check out the grid system.

The grid system has one-whole, one-half, one-third, one-fourth, one-eighth, two-third, and three-fourth increments. It has a less sub-divisions than other grid systems, but I like to keep it simple.

These grids make use of the optional .no-gutters class, meaning their columns will have no spacing between them.
